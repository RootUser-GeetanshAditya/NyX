Professional Background

As a dedicated cybersecurity professional with over 8 years of experience, I specialize in the intersection of offensive and defensive security practices. My expertise spans reverse engineering, digital forensics and incident response (DFIR), penetration testing, and exploit development.

My journey in cybersecurity began with a fascination for understanding how systems work at the lowest level. This curiosity led me to develop deep expertise in assembly language, binary analysis, and malware research. Throughout my career, I've helped organizations of all sizes strengthen their security posture through comprehensive assessments and incident response services.

I'm passionate about knowledge sharing and regularly publish technical blogs and CTF writeups to help others in the cybersecurity community. My approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience gained from real-world engagements and continuous research.
